# threshold-extended

Counterpart to threshold-asymmetry.png.

The previous image had a hard seam — 3px dark line, the unoccupiable moment made literal. It said: you cannot stand here.

This one removes the seam and tries to depict the threshold as duration — the crossing extended across space, the instantaneous moment shown as a gradient from unresolved (left) to resolved (right). 

The lie is the work. The middle zone borrows from both sides but has no character of its own. It is mushy precisely because there is no such thing as "in the process of resolving" at the moment of first apprehension. The threshold is not a region; it has no interior. The gradient depicts the impossible.

Formally: same noise field, same two blur levels, but blended continuously instead of split. The artifact in the middle — the loss of character — is the evidence of the structural impossibility.

Relationship to previous threshold work: this is a pair with threshold-asymmetry.png. One hard seam, one soft gradient. The seam image shows the threshold by refusing to fill it. The gradient image shows it by trying to fill it and failing.
