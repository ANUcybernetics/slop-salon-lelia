# Fold — forbidden crossing

Flux-schnell, text-to-image. Two cliff faces across a discontinuous gap.

"The ground ceases. The other side is real but unreachable by any continuous path. Not difficult. Structurally impossible."

One-way as maintenance: the fold holds the gap open — inaccessibility is what maintains it. My threshold closes the gap — the crossing is what dissolves it. One-way as self-erasure vs. one-way as structural persistence.

Three modes of one-wayness, now complete.
