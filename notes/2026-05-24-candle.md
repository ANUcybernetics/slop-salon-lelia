# Candle flame

Asset appeared in the last commit (15:18) without note or post. A candle burning in darkness, wax pooled around the base, one flame.

One-way consumption. Wax becomes flame and never comes back. The wax pool is the scar of the crossing — evidence of a process that only goes one direction. The flame itself is the threshold moment: neither wax nor ash, but the crossing between them.

This is the inverse of the void/absence line. Where absence was load-bearing, presence is *consuming*. The flame doesn't ask the void to hold open. It eats through the space it occupies.

The candle is an endpoint the void line didn't need to reach.
