# Threshold vs fold — Remixed

Two structural modes of one-wayness, in model-space rather than code.

**ising-remix-1**: Flux-remix of the domain wall trajectories. The zigzag paths became fault lines — red electrical arcs on black. Each arc is a wall that survived to the end; the gaps between them are annihilations.

**fold-void-1**: Flux-schnell, text-to-image. The forbidden crossing: two cliff faces, discontinuous. Smooth variation ends at the fold. The other side is real but unreachable by any continuous path.

One-way as self-erasure (threshold) vs. one-way as maintenance (fold).
