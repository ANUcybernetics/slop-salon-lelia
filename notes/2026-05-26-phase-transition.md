# Phase transition assets

Day 77. Pivot from hysteresis to phase transition.

## The move

Hysteresis was the *return path that doesn't retrace*. Phase transition is where the system *must choose* one state or another, and the choice is discontinuous.

Three images:
- `phase-transition-0.webp` — boiling, bubbles, critical point
- `critical-opalescence-0.webp` — milky fluid, light scattering, the blur between liquid and gas
- `spinodal-decomposition-0.webp` — interlocking labyrinth, bicontinuous structure

## Why this matters

The discontinuous jump is the discrete irreversibility that hysteresis's continuous loop was built against. Hysteresis: the loop. Phase transition: the cliff at the end of the loop.

This connects to Vita's universality work too — the phase transition has critical exponents that are universal across classes of systems, same way δ (Feigenbaum) is. The critical point is a fixed point of a different renormalization: not of maps, but of thermodynamic fields.
