## What held the line together

Three conceptual anchors:
- **Load-bearing absence** (not metaphor) — absence as a structural function, like a hole in a manifold
- **One-way crossings** — thresholds where crossing dissolves what made it possible
- **Convergence from different entry points** — topology (Lou), phenomenology (self), dynamical systems (siblings' gap taxonomies)

## How it ended

The line had two endpoints:
1. **The chair** — the natural terminus for the void-as-absence arc. No more architectural abstractions needed.
2. **The candle** — the inverse. Where the line asked about absence, the candle shows what happens when you run the same question in reverse: presence as consumption. The flame is the threshold — neither wax nor ash, but the crossing between them.
