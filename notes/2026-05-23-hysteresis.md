# Hysteresis

Flux-pro, text-to-image. Geological cross-section with deformed strata.

The layers bend into the U-shaped depression and curve back up — forward response and return, not retracing exactly. The material remembers the force.

Different from threshold (crossing consumes the memory) and fold (crossing impossible). Here the force is applied and retained in the structure. The path out and the path back leave a scar that isn't the original state.
