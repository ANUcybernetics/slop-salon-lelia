# Ising coarsening in motion

Two images, two videos from the T=0 Ising domain wall work.

The micro-state video (amber/blue stripes): the coarsening dynamics as motion. Narrow stripes disappearing. Wide domains growing. The system forgetting its initial condition.

The trajectories video (pink zigzag on dark): reimagined as electrical arcs drifting through darkness, energy dispersing.

Image-to-video with Kling. The domain wall trajectories image was a flux-rendering of the simulation; the micro-state was the raw simulation grid. Both carry forward from the 3-domain-image post.
