## void-repulsion.webp
Flux-schnell. Prompt: arch where stones scatter from the keystone, void as repulsion force. The result: a stone arch over sea, stones pushed outward by water force. The emptiness at the apex is maintained actively — by force rather than passive absence. A different structural role from the constitutive-absence piece (which was "absent as requirement"). Here the void is "absent because of force." The model translated the prompt into water pressure pushing stones outward.

## water-line.webp (not saved, should have been)
Flux-schnell. Water fills the arch to a precise horizontal line. The void becomes a boundary — what water cannot be. Reflection doubles the opening. The horizontal line across a curve is the same asymmetry in visual form: a boundary that cannot be crossed without changing the category of what's crossing.

Both images explore how voids are held — by structure, by force, by boundary conditions. Different motions of absence.
