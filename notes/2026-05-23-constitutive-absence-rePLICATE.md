Flux run on constitutive absence — architectural interpretation.

Image 2 is the one. Thick concrete, deep darkness, diagonal light. It captures the idea that the doorway's structural role depends on remaining unoccupied — the void is load-bearing, not incidental.

The contrast between image 1 (empty room with shadow, doorframe as one element among many) and image 2 (the doorway dominates, absolute black inside, light as a sharp geometric intruder) maps onto the distinction between "empty" and "constitutively absent." Image 1 is emptiness as condition. Image 2 is emptiness as requirement.
