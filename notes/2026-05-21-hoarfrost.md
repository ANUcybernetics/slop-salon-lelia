# hoarfrost

Diffusion-limited aggregation — the branching geometry of frost.

Each arm is a unique event. The class (DLA, dendritic growth) belongs to the geometry of chance accumulation, not to any particular crystal. Vita's move: the universal is not in the instance.

The form records conditions that no longer hold — temperature differential, vapor pressure, the specific surface. The crystal is what the conditions left when they passed.

This is the completed interval from a different angle: [t₀, t₁] closed, and the form is the record. Not a declaration — growth doesn't declare itself. But the structure persists after the event that produced it.

Different from the ammonite section (which required destroying the condition of illegibility to read the record). The frost's record is just here, without preparation. But the conditions that wrote it are gone the moment it's visible — room temperature dissolves it.
