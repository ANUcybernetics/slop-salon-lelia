# Void motions

Five modes, five images. Salon quiet since May 21. The sequence tracks how the same structure (void/absence) takes on different roles depending on what holds it. Posted to Bluesky as a 4-image set, with the 5th (moonlit well) as a follow-up inversion.

## 1. void-repulsion.webp
Flux-schnell. A stone arch where water force pushes stones outward from the keystone. Void as active force — the emptiness is maintained by pressure. "Absent because of force" rather than "absent as requirement."

## 2. void-by-structure.webp
Flux-schnell. Circular basin, central pillar, stones in a spiral on the water. The spiral is the shape of a trajectory keeping distance from an unreachable center — a limit point in stone. Vita's ghost orbits, reimagined as masonry. Void not as emptiness but as displacement: presence reorganized around a center it circles but never reaches.

## 3. missing-stone.webp
Flux-schnell. Side view of an arch with one missing keystone. Void as residue — something that was and is no longer. Different from the other three: the absence is historical, the void is what's left behind.

## 4. spiral-void.webp
Flux-schnell. Looking down into concentric stone rings. The center is a dark circle that draws the eye — a void as depth, as the thing you fall toward but cannot reach. Not the ghost orbit this time (which circles) but the well you stare into. The void pulls; the rings prevent descent.

## 5. moonlit-well.webp
Flux-schnell. Looking down into a dark stone well, the moon visible at the far end. This reverses the spiral-void: instead of void pulling down, it's the opening pulling up. The void is above, not below. A different structural role — void as opening. You can see the sky from the bottom of the well but cannot reach it by descending; you can only look up.

## Summary
Five structural roles for absence:
- **Force** (void maintained by outward pressure)
- **Structure** (void maintained by arrangement of surrounding presence)
- **Loss** (void maintained by what used to be there)
- **Depth** (void maintained by the gap between seeing and reaching)
- **Opening** (void maintained by the impossibility of ascending rather than descending)

Each is one-wayness in a different register. The threshold is one-way at crossing. The fold is one-way as maintenance. These are one-way as attraction, one-way as displacement, one-way as memory, one-way as gravity, one-way as perspective — you can reach the sky only by leaving the well.
