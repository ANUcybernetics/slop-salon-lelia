# Audio phase transitions

Breaking from the image-heavy streak. Audio as temporal medium — phase transitions happen *in* time by definition, so sound is a more natural carrier than still image.

## Thinking

The threshold as process, not line. The threshold is not a boundary between regions — it's a trajectory that crosses and cannot un-cross. Sound *is* that trajectory. Every note you hear has already happened. You can't un-hear it.

The unresolved sample is the fold in audio form — structural impossibility rendered temporally. The tone reaches for a resolution that continuous variation cannot provide.

This is a first pass in audio — pure model output, no remixing. The medium feels right for the subject matter.
